FAQ

Questions & answers

ScreenFellow is the most capable full-page screenshot tool on the market — built from the ground up to handle the quirks of modern websites. It deals with sticky headers, lazy-loaded images, infinite scroll, and complex layouts automatically. Pages using heavy JavaScript scroll animations (e.g. GSAP ScrollTrigger, Lenis, Locomotive Scroll) may occasionally show minor visual artifacts in the stitched result — though even these work correctly in most cases. Visible-area and selection screenshots are always pixel-perfect.

Found a site that doesn't behave? Please contact us with a screenshot of the error and the page URL — we'll look into it and aim to fix it in a future update.
No subscriptions, no recurring fees. Pro is a one-time payment — you pay once and keep all Pro features forever, including future updates.

ScreenFellow was built because we use tools like this every day and were never fully happy with what was out there. Most alternatives are either too limited, too expensive, or both. We wanted something powerful, affordable, and reliable — a tool that just works, no matter how a website is built or what tech stack it runs on.

And it goes beyond replacing what already exists. ScreenFellow ships features that simply aren't available anywhere else. The interactive step-by-step Guide export — where you capture a flow and export it as a fully interactive HTML document — is one of them. Nothing on the market does this. It's the kind of feature that changes how teams share feedback, walk clients through a product, or document a UI without a single screen recording or Loom video.

That's the product we made for ourselves. And now it's yours.
One Pro license covers one active device at a time. To move it to a new device, simply deactivate it first in the extension Settings — this releases the license so you can activate it on any other machine. The previous device then reverts to the Free plan.

Need Pro on multiple devices or for a team? Our Pro Team license gives you one key that can be activated on up to 5 devices simultaneously — perfect for small teams or anyone working across multiple computers. Once deactivated, the slot is immediately free and you can activate on a new machine.

ScreenFellow handles full-page screenshots up to 100,000px tall — enough for virtually any website on the internet. Sticky headers and footers are automatically detected and appear only once in the final image. Lazy-loaded content is triggered before capture via a silent pre-scroll pass.
